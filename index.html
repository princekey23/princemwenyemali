my journey

DRC

Bukavu DRC
Being born and raised in the Democratic Republic of Congo isn't easy at all. We try to survive in any mean but our Country still pushing us back through hardship. Corruption has devastated the economy of our country at the level where 80% of the youth remain unemployed. The country is rich in mineral resources but the population is poor. As a citizen I tried to hustle around the Country with no success so I moved from my hometown of Bukavu to Uvira, from Uvira it was still the same so I moved to Lubumbashi until I decided to head to South Africa.

Uganda

kampala CBD
Uganda is wonderful place to live, But as it said that in every african nation, the major problem is access to employment. I guess that's true because even the Country that us in Congo consider richer than us are facing some common challenges as those in Congo. Beside that I learnt to better use English vocabulary in Uganda so that's what helped me to survive in Kenya when I left Uganda.

Kenya

Nairobi CBD
Wow, I am so excited to talk about Kenya. From all countries i've been to, Kenya still attract me. I love Mombasa. People are very generous even if they were advising me not to trust them. Kenya is a country that trying it's best to help citizens and the riches love the Country. So Kenya is far better than all East African countries including DRC. Kenyan motivated me and gave courage to work hard because they showed me that PHD doesn't get you a job in Africa but your thinking capacity is what help you but you need to work hard. So kenyan are the most creative people from all the African countries that I've travelled to. Thank you Kenya for giving me confidence and focus on what I want to do.

Zambia

Lusaka CBD
Zambia is another Country where people willing to help strangers even if they are poor. Zambian are generous but they are addicted to drink. You find a lot of people drunk early morning and that's what upset me. beside that Zambia is a place to make money if you are a hard worker. For physical works, you won't struggle is Zambia.

Tanzania

Dar es salam CBD
Tanzania is my choice. Living in Tanzania was the best period of my ife. And I enjoyed the way people interact each other. wow, nice beaches (not maintained as in South Africa but they are beautiful), so there is a lot to explore and a lot to learn about the tanzanian society.

South Africa

Cape Town CBD
South Africa is the richest and the most developed african country. It is by far the industrial power house of Africa. Beside being rich, South Africa have beautiful and generous people. Even the picture of SA in other african Countries and all over the world, was painted to be the most xenophobic country but South Africa is beyond that. People in South Africa are very tolerant and patient. So even if some of them a violent but still a big percentage of South African are against those xenophobic acts that circulate around the country sometimes. So don't be afraid because you will never see the beauty of this country in other african countries.

Rwanda, Burundi

Kigali CBD

Rwanda

Rwanda is beautiful Country but as I grew up visiting it, I saw nothing special than what is in my Country. So Rwanda is the safest country from all the countries I visited. No pick pocketing or gangsterism. So it's by far th country that almost every one respect the law. Rwanda is beautiful!

Burundi

Being one of the poorest countries in the world, doesn't stop Burundi from showing and sharing its natural beauty with foreigners and visitors. Being in Burundi makes you feel at home away from home. so Burundi is tourist friendly almost like kenya and tanzanania.
